Introduction

Yanglix is the sacred language gifted to the Kypwatchidwax by King Pwatchidwax. When the King spoke the First Word, He gave us language. That language was Yanglix.

The Phonological Inventory and Orthography

Yanglix uses a very distinct phonological inventory. When King Pwatchidwax spoke the First Word, He only spoke in consonants. So, Yanglix more commonly uses hard consonants rather than vowels or liquid consonants.

Phonotactics and Suprasegmentals

Introductory Syntax

There is no "clear" sentence order in Yanglix. Anyone can say any sentnece in any order and context will help the sentence move along. It is generally recommended for someone to stick to the same sentence order for the entire duration of their speech.

Examples:

ya xakkybya
ya xakky-bya
1SG happy-NEG
“I am sad.”
